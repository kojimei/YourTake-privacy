# YourTake-privacy# YourTake Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 5, 2026**

## Information We Collect
YourTake collects the following information when you use our app:
- Email address (for account creation)
- Username (chosen by you)
- Posts and votes you create within the app
- Profile avatar images you upload

## How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
- Create and maintain your account
- Display your posts and votes to other users
- Calculate your points and level progress
- Provide personalized news feed recommendations

## Data Storage
Your data is stored securely using Supabase. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## Third Party Services
YourTake uses the following third party services:
- Supabase (database and authentication)
- GIPHY (GIF search in comments)
- NewsAPI / RSS feeds (news content)
